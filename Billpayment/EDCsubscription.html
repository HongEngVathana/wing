Pay your bills anywhere with your Wing Bank App! Paying your bills doesn’t need to be a hassle. Pay your bills quickly, conveniently, and securely with the Wing Bank App. You don't even need to leave your house. Wing has partnered with over 200 billers, allowing you to pay your utilities, loans, suppliers, and more with ease. Pay your bills on time straight from your phone. You can even schedule your bills to be paid automatically as needed right from your fingertips.

Key Benefit

Security

Safe and secure transactions with advanced encryption, ensuring your information and money are protected. 

Time-Saving

No more queues; manage everything from your phone, anytime, anywhere.

Automation

You can schedule your regular bills with ease anytime from your fingertips.

Accessibility

Available 24/7 on your mobile device, making it easy to pay bills at your convenience.

Convenience

Pay multiple bills from a single platform without leaving your home. Wing Bank offers a variety of digital services through its app and a network of over 11,000 agents nationwide.

How It Works

  1. Open Wing Bank App: Launch the app on your mobile device.
  2. Select “Bill Payment”: Navigate to the bill payment section.
  3. Select Bill Type or Search for Your Biller: Choose the type of bill you want to pay (e.g., Utilities, Finance, etc.).
  4. Select Company: Choose the specific biller from the list of over 200 partners.
  5. Fill in Information: Provide the required details such as account number or bill reference number.
  6. Click “Pay”: Initiate the payment process.
  7. Enter PIN Code and Click “Confirm”: Verify your payment with your PIN, and you're done!
  8. Receive Notification: You will receive a confirmation notification on the app.

How To Start

1. Download the Wing Bank App: Available on both iOS and Android.

2. Open Account/Log In: Open an account or log in if you already have one.
