What is Wingpoints?

Wingpoints - the coolest customer loyalty program in Cambodia is finally here! Now you can earn Wingpoints when you pay for your dining, shopping, traveling, and more with WingPay QR/Wing Bank KHQR

How to earn Wingpoints?

Earn Wingpoints

Paying for your shopping, dinning, traveling, and more via Wingpay or Wing KHQR.

Get rewarded

Get Wingpoints back for every amount you spend based on the merchant participation.

Wingpoints validity period

Your Wingpoints will be valid for 12 months (starting from the earning date).

How to redeem Wingpoints?

Wingmall has more than 1,000 well-known merchants that sell thousands of items, including, food, drinks, groceries, electronics, toys, beauty products and many other goods.

  • How do you redeem with your Wingpoints?

    You can pay with your Wingpoints at any of the participating merchant outlets by scanning the Wing Bank QR code.

  • Where can I collect Wingpoints?

    You can collect Wingpoints and pay them inside Wingmall for any of the 100,000+ SKUs that are available.

  • Can I pay my Wingpoints for Phone Top Up?

    You can also pay your Wingpoints for Phone Top Up.

  • Can I earn Wingpoints even when I paid?

    You will earn Wingpoints even when you redeem them at any of the participating merchants –more rewards on top of rewards!
